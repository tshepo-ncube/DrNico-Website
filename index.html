Narrative therapy was developed within the Post-Modern era of therapy and counselling. The objective of the model is to understand the discourses that exist in individual's lives which contribute to their life story. Through identifying the narrative, our highly trained staff is able to highlight negative narratives which are impacting the individual and hindering their personal growth

Although our service is aimed at humanistic improvement, sometimes people struggle with certain challenges which require in-depth therapy. Psychodynamic therapy is a therapeutic model which aims to assist individuals in instances where in-depth therapy is needed. This model of therapy is particularly aimed at alleviating any maladaptive concern people may present with, which includes but is not limited to anxiety difficulties, adjustment difficulties, relationship, identity and sexual orientation. We offer therapeutic service in this regard but an assessment must be done to review the appropriateness of intervention offered.

At Acendify, our partners are well-trained in medical-related requirements for mediation example: in cases of negligence or disputes. Our trained partners also provide mediation in parental disputes.

Narrative counselling is a brief model aimed at achieving outcomes in a short space of time. Once we understand your needs as a company, and assess your staffing totals and additional factors such as possible EAP services, we are then able to generate the appropriate service.

With individual counselling, we offer a 15 min free consult, to ascertain if our model is suited to your needs. If it is a match, we will require a 1 session asssement whereby at the end we can map out how many intervention sessions will be needed. If our model does not align with your needs, Acendify will refer you to an appropriate service where you are able to access the appropriate services needed.
